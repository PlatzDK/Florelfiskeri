About Florel Fiskeri

Florel Fiskeri is a cozy and modern cottage, tucked away in the woods by Denmark’s renowned Karup River. Perfect for families, couples, or keen anglers, the cottage sleeps up to 6 and offers all the comfort you need for a relaxing getaway.

  • • 2 bedrooms with double beds
  • • Open-plan kitchen & living area
  • • Bathroom with shower
  • • Terrace with views of the lake
  • • Free Wi-Fi & smart TV
  • • Drying cabinet for fishing gear
  • • No smoking, pets by prior arrangement

Experience the Area

Located in the beautiful Kompedal Forest, Florel Fiskeri offers direct access to unspoiled nature and some of Denmark’s best trout fishing:

  • Karup River: Famous for big sea trout and fly fishing
  • Forest hiking trails & mountain biking
  • Abundant wildlife – deer, birds, squirrels
  • Swim or paddle in the nearby lake
  • Just a short drive to Karup or historic Viborg
